Taxonomy Orientation

What is a Taxonomy?

A taxonomy is a classification system used to organize and categorize information. Itâ€™s a way of grouping related concepts together based on characteristics, attributes, or properties usually in a hierarchical structure. It is meant to help improve accessibility to information.

When do you use it?

Taxonomies can be used in content creation to ensure consistency and accuracy in the use of terminology and facilitate the reuse of content across different platforms.

Why should you use it?

Organizing information into a taxonomy, makes it easier to search for and retrieve relevant content. A well-designed taxonomy can help users quickly find the information they need.
